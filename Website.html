About G.P Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital was established in 1956, making it one of the oldest technical institutes in Uttarakhand. G.P. Nainital is widely recognized as one of the best institutions for technical education in the state. The college offers a variety of diploma programs catering to multiple engineering and technical fields. Its curriculum is thoughtfully designed to bridge the gap between theoretical academics and hands-on industry skills. The institute emphasizes both classroom learning and practical training to prepare students for real-world challenges. G.P. Nainital boasts a strong faculty team comprising experienced educators and industry professionals. Industry experts frequently collaborate with the college to provide students with up-to-date knowledge and training. The college maintains a vast and active alumni network spread across top industries in India and abroad. Students benefit from mentorship, internships, and placement support through this network. Government Polytechnic Nainital continues to empower future engineers and technocrats with quality technical education.





Admission
The Admission process steps are mentioned here.

  • Qualified Jeep Examination with under 1500 rank.
  • Offline Admission process in the campus after declaration of jeep result for 1 month.
  • Online Counselling.
  • College Allotment and Branch Allotment Or Seat Allotment.
  • UBTER Offline and Online registration and Some little offline Process.
  • Document Verification.
  • Offline fee submission.




    • Courses Offered

    G.P Nainital offers diploma in various fields.

    Diploma in Civil Engineering

    Focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects. Covers surveying, structural analysis, and construction management.

    Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

    Deals with the principles of mechanics, design, manufacturing, and thermal engineering. Equips students for careers in various industrial sectors.

    Diploma in Electrical Engineering

    Covers the generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical power, as well as electronic circuits and control systems.

    Diploma in Information Technology

    Focuses on programming, data structures, algorithms, database management, and computer networks. Prepares students for the IT industry.

    Diploma in Electronics Engineering

    Focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of electronic devices and systems. Includes circuits, microprocessors, communication systems, and digital electronics.

    Diploma in Modern Office Management

    Covers office procedures, communication skills, computer applications, record management, and automation tools for efficient office administration.

    Diploma in Pharmacy

    Provides essential knowledge of pharmacy practices, drug formulation, pharmacology, and healthcare. Prepares students to work as pharmacists in various sectors.

      • History
  • Establishment Year: Some sources report 1957, others 1958 or 1975. The earliest mention is 1957. Maximum Article mentioned Establishment year in 1975.
  • Previous Name: Formerly known as Nainital Polytechnic..
  • Affiliation: Initially with UP Technical Board, Lucknow; now with Uttarakhand Technical Education Board, Roorkee, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
  • Early Years: One of the first polytechnics established in Uttarakhand.
  • Courses Offered: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics, Information Technology, Pharmacy, and Modern Office Management & Secretarial Practice.
  • A long-standing technical education institute serving the region since the late 1950s.

  • Accessibility: Nainital is accessible by road from Haldwani (about 40 km away), the nearest railway station, and from Pantnagar Airport.
  • Coordinates: Approximate geographical coordinates of Nainital are 29.38°N latitude and 79.46°E longitude.
